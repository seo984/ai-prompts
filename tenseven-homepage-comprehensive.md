## 3. Foundational Semantic Sections: Understanding Private Security in NZ
In New Zealand, private security is a highly regulated sector overseen by the **Private Security Personnel Licensing Authority (PSPLA)**. Understanding the different classifications of security allows you to choose the exact level of protection your property requires.

### Property Guards vs. Mobile Patrols
*   **Static Property Guards:** These are "fixed-site" professionals who remain on your premises for a set duration (e.g., an 8-hour overnight shift). They are ideal for construction sites, gated industrial complexes, and corporate lobbies where constant physical presence is required.
*   **Mobile Patrols:** This service involves a security officer in a marked vehicle visiting your site at randomized intervals. It serves as a strong visual deterrent and is often the most cost-effective solution for multiple small-to-medium businesses in a centralized area like Mt Roskill.

### Crowd Controllers and Personal Guards
*   **Crowd Controllers:** Specifically trained for events and retail environments where high-volume foot traffic needs management and de-escalation expertise.
*   **Personal Guards:** Focused on the individual protection of high-profile clients or persons facing specific safety threats.
